Quick Construction lets you create simple architectural marvels at light speed! 
Quickly construct your builds by moving a Cursor around the world to help you visualize prop placement with grid-snapping.

How to use:
use the ARROW keys to move Cursor around the world
use Page Up and Page Down to raise/lower the Cursor
press ENTER to Place a prop
press E to rotate the build around the Origin
press O to move the build Origin to the Cursor
press Y to reset the Cursor to the Origin
